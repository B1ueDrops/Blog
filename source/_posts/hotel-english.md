## 词汇

* reservation: 预约
* single/double room: 单人/双人间
* XX room with an ocean view: 海景房
* XX room with a garden view: 园景房
* non-smoking room: 无烟房
* breakfast tickets: 早餐票
* room card: 房卡
* pull up your reservation: 工作人员查看你的预约信息
* deposit: 押金
* amenity: 便利设施



## 句子

> 我以XX为名字订了房间.

I have a reservatiion under XX.
