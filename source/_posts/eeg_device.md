## International 10-20 system

![International 10-20 system (21电极)](./eeg_device/10_20_system.svg)

* Nasion: 鼻根
* Inion: 枕骨隆突
* Fp: pre-frontal lobe, 前额叶
* F: frontal lobe, 额叶
* T: temporal lobe, 颞叶, 头颅两侧靠近耳朵的地方
* P: parietal lobe, 顶叶
* O: occipital lobe, 枕叶
* C: center
* Fz, Cz, Pz: 用于参考.
* 奇数编号在左, 偶数编号在右.
